Why you should move to Norway

Why Norway?

  1. Ranked as one of the best countries to live
  2. Lowest crime rates in the world
  3. One of the only countries you can regularly see the northern lights
  4. If you like the outdoors, Norway is perfect for you. Perfect, for hiking, camping, skiing and fishing!
  5. And if that wasn't enough, Norway has free healthcare, education and college tuition

The Coronavirus

A lot of people steer clear of certain countries due to their COVID-19 status. However, since March 2020, Norway has had less than 1,000 deaths due to COVID-19. 70% of Norway's population is fully vaccinated and 80% have at least 1 dose.
